About Warner Equipment

A2 Pressure Vessel Manufacturer — Subsidiary of Warner Group

Founded in June 2021 with registered capital of 128 million RMB, Jiangsu Warner Petrochemical Equipment Technology Co., Ltd. is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Jiangsu Warner Petrochemical Engineering Group Co., Ltd. The company holds an A2 fixed pressure vessel manufacturing license (certified by China Chemical Equipment Association, valid through December 2029).

We specialize in chemical separation and purification technology, focusing on core equipment R&D and manufacturing for biodegradable materials (PBAT/PBS/PGA/PLA). Our products serve petrochemical, coal chemical, fine chemical, and biodegradable material industries.
